Help : How to use this App

This App shows a marker that is your home. To set where home it, go to preferences and Type in a valid location you call home.


Please Note, if the location is invalid You will be altered and asked to enter a valid location.


This application Requrires an Internet Connection to Find you, and Find your Home, If you are not connected to a network, the map will not be available for you.


You must have Google Services to use this application, otherwise you will be prompted to enable them.



Change your preferences to your desire, to get your favourite view of Home
